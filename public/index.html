What is MEV Shield?

MEV Shield is a Cloudflare Worker that protects your crypto transactions from being stolen by bots.

What is MEV?

MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) is when bots steal money from regular crypto traders. Here's how it works:

The Problem:
  1. You want to buy crypto - You place an order to buy tokens
  2. Bots see your order - They can see your transaction before it's processed
  3. Bots jump ahead - They buy the same tokens first, driving up the price
  4. You pay more - Your order goes through at the higher price
  5. Bots sell and profit - They sell immediately, keeping the difference

Result: You lose money, bots make money. This happens to millions of traders every day.

How MEV Shield Protects You:

üõ°Ô∏è The Solution:
  1. Private channels - Your transaction goes through private, hidden routes
  2. Bots can't see it - They don't know your transaction exists
  3. No front-running - Bots can't jump ahead of you
  4. You get fair prices - Your orders execute at the intended price
  5. Keep your money - No more stolen profits

Result: You trade safely, bots can't steal from you.

Technical Architecture:

  • JSON-RPC Proxy: Intercepts wallet requests and intelligently routes them
  • Private Relay Integration: Routes write transactions through Flashbots, bloXroute, and Eden networks
  • Fallback Protection: Automatic failover between multiple private relays
  • Read Operation Optimization: Direct forwarding to public RPCs for balance checks
  • Transaction Tracking: Unique ID generation and status monitoring
